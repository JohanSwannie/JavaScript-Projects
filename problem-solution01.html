JavaScript Problem Solving - Author: Johan Swan

Problem Description 1

 

        Johan Swan & Richard Swan each own a restaurant of their own. Both have
        a rule of rates in place according to which tips are paid towards waiters
        which will be added to the customer bill. Given arrays of the last 5 bills
        for each restaurant as well as fixed tip rates for each bill amount are the
        given input data. Calculate for each restaurant what the tip will be for each
        bill as well as the amount of the final bills. Then calculate the average of
        all the tips for each restaurant. Display whether Johan or Richard pays
        the highest average amount of tips to waiters.

Problem Description 2

 

        The 2 given arrays are 6 persons and their birth year respectively.
        Use callback functions in Javascript to detemine each person's age,
        their average heart rate and whether they are full of age(18 or older).
